Sri Dhalada Maligawa

The Sri Dalada Maligawa or The Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic is a temple in the city of Kandy in Sri Lanka. It was built within the royal palace complex which houses the tooth relic of the Buddha, a tooth, which is venerated by Buddhists.

Koneswaram Kovil

The most sacred of the Pancha Ishwarams of Sri Lanka, it was built significantly during the ancient period on top of Konesar Malai, a promontory overlooking Trincomalee District, Gokarna bay and the Indian Ocean. The monument contains its main shrine to Shiva in the form Kona-Eiswara, shortened to Konesar.

Why you should visit Sri Lanka?

Visiting Sri Lanka is an enchanting experience that immerses you in a world of natural wonders and vibrant cultural heritage. From the golden shores of its pristine beaches to the misty peaks of its majestic mountains, this island paradise offers a diverse tapestry of breathtaking landscapes. Explore ancient cities steeped in history, marvel at magnificent temples, and wander through colonial fortresses. Encounter a rich tapestry of wildlife in lush national parks, from elephants and leopards to exotic birds. The warm hospitality of the Sri Lankan people will make you feel right at home as you savor the flavors of their delicious cuisine. Whether you seek adventure, relaxation, or cultural immersion, Sri Lanka beckons with its affordability, beauty, and a wealth of unforgettable experiences.

Cheapest flights to Sri Lanka.

Several airlines offer flights to Sri Lanka from around the world. SriLankan Airlines, the national carrier of Sri Lanka, operates flights to Colombo from numerous international destinations. Emirates, Qatar Airways, Etihad Airways, Air India, Singapore Airlines, and Thai Airways are also popular choices, providing connections to Colombo from their respective hubs. These airlines offer convenient options for travelers coming from different parts of the world, allowing you to reach Sri Lanka with ease. It's advisable to check with the airlines or use flight comparison websites to find the most up-to-date and affordable flights to Sri Lanka based on your departure location.
